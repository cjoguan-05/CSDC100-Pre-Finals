Analogy on the Use of AI in Development Work

A wedding scenario that shows how tools support, but people still shape the final result.

Wedding Scenario

A wedding documentation team charges ₱40,000 for their services. They will use an iPhone 16 Pro Max to cover the event and CapCut for the same-day edit video. The team has 2 years of experience and an above-average portfolio.

Question: Would you agree to that deal? Explain your answer and justify the integrity of their work. Then, reflect on the integrity, creativity, and skill level of “vibe” coders who use AI.

Why the tools do not define the value

  • The camera and edit app are only part of the service, not the whole value.
  • Framing, timing, and story decide how the final video feels.
  • Portfolio, reliability, and client care matter more than the brand of the gear.
  • Different couples want different styles, so one setup will not fit every vision.

Parallels to AI assisted development

  • AI tools for code are like the phone and edit app, they support the developer.
  • The developer still designs the system and chooses which suggestions to accept.
  • The shipped software is like the final wedding video that the human team owns.
  • Users judge reliability and clarity of the product, not which AI tools helped.

Personal response and analysis

I would say no for my own wedding because I want a certain look for the video. I want a cinematic feel, and an iPhone and CapCut setup cannot give that style. That is a personal preference, not a judgment of their skills. I would still consider them for other projects. Their work looks clean and consistent. They know how to get the right shots and edit fast. They stay honest about what they can deliver. They show real samples in their portfolio and do not promise results they cannot achieve. That shows integrity because they stay true to their skill level instead of overselling their service. “Vibe” coders who use AI are similar. AI can make coding faster, but the coder is still responsible for the final result. A good coder checks the AI output, fixes errors, and makes sure the code works. Integrity means taking ownership of the final work. Creativity shows in how they guide the AI to get the right solution. Skill shows in how they solve issues, clean the code, and make it stable.
Key insight: tools and AI can speed up work, but integrity, judgment, and skill still come from the person using them.
